About HojaTrail

HojaTrail is a nature-themed map experience designed to help users discover nearby bookstores, libraries, and peaceful places.

The goal is to blend technology with nature — making exploration feel organic, calm, and intuitive.

Built with 🌿 passion for reading, nature, and design.
